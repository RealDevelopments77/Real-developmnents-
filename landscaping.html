Our Services

Landscaping

Hard and soft landscaping designed to last. From a simple patio to a full multi-level garden scheme.

Landscaping That Works From the Ground Up

Good landscaping starts underground. Drainage, sub-base preparation and levels: if these aren't right, the finished landscape won't be either. That's where Real Developments Ltd is different: we're a groundworks company first, which means every landscaping project we deliver is built on a properly prepared, well-drained base that will last for decades.

We work with homeowners across Sevenoaks, Tonbridge, Tunbridge Wells and the wider Kent area, delivering both hard and soft landscaping schemes, from a new patio or driveway to a fully designed and constructed garden. Whether you want something low-maintenance and contemporary or lush and planted, we have the skills and experience to make it happen.

Types of Landscaping We Do

Hard Landscaping

Patios, driveways, pathways, steps and retaining walls. We lay sandstone, porcelain, block paving, gravel and resin-bound surfaces, always on a properly prepared sub-base with correct drainage and falls. Hard landscaping is the backbone of any garden design, and we get it right every time.

Soft Landscaping

Planting schemes, turf laying, raised beds, hedging and tree planting. We work with the natural layout of the garden to create planted areas that complement the hard landscaping, balancing low-maintenance ground cover with seasonal interest and structure.

Raised Beds & Borders

Sleeper raised beds, brick or block-built borders and retaining planters. Whether you want a kitchen garden, a formal planted border or a contemporary raised vegetable bed, we build to a solid spec that will hold its shape and look great for years without movement or deterioration.

Garden Structures & Features

Steps, retaining walls, water features, pergola bases and outbuilding pads. We handle all the groundworks and construction elements that make a landscaping scheme function properly. Your garden won't just look beautiful; it'll be structurally sound and built to last.

Every landscaping project starts with a proper site assessment. We look at drainage, levels, access, soil conditions and your brief before we price or plan anything. This means the quote you get is accurate, the design is practical, and the finished result is exactly what you had in mind.
