What types of foundations do you lay in Tonbridge?

We lay strip foundations, trench-fill foundations, raft foundations and pad foundations across Tonbridge and Kent. Foundation type is determined by site conditions, soil classification and structural loading. We advise on the right approach for each individual project.

Do you handle the excavation and concrete pour together in Tonbridge?

Yes. We manage both the excavation and the concrete pour as a combined scope. This removes the risk of programme gaps between contractors and ensures the excavation profile is correct before any concrete is placed. It also means consistent quality control throughout.

How deep do foundations need to be in Tonbridge?

Foundation depth in Tonbridge depends on soil type, tree proximity and structural loading. The local clay soils in many areas of Tonbridge require foundations at 1m or deeper to reach stable bearing ground. We assess each site individually and work to the engineer's specification to ensure depth and bearing capacity are correct.
