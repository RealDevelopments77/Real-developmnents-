External Restoration Specialists in Tunbridge Wells

The exterior of your property is the first thing anyone sees. Over time, render cracks and stains, paintwork fades and peels, and mortar joints erode: leaving a property looking worn, tired and uninviting. Our external restoration service addresses all of this properly, bringing your property's exterior back to the standard it deserves.

We work with homeowners across Tunbridge Wells, Tonbridge, Sevenoaks and the wider Kent area, tackling everything from a straightforward repaint on a rendered semi to a full repoint and render repair on a period Victorian or Edwardian property. Tunbridge Wells has a high proportion of older properties with original brickwork and lime mortar, and we have significant experience working sensitively on these homes. Every job is assessed thoroughly before work begins, we identify and resolve the underlying issues first, rather than simply painting over problems.

What Our Restoration Service Includes

External Repainting

We repaint rendered, pebbledash and painted brick properties using high-quality masonry paint systems. Work includes surface preparation, filling and patching, priming where required, and two coats of premium masonry paint, leaving a clean, even, weather-resistant finish that protects the property for years.

Render Repair & Restoration

Cracked or blown render lets water in and looks poor. We cut out failed sections, apply the correct sand and cement or modern polymer render, and blend the repair into the surrounding surface. Where properties need more extensive re-rendering, we can tackle full elevations to a consistent, professional finish.

Brick Repointing

Old and eroded mortar joints allow water penetration that damages brickwork and internal structures over time. We rake out deteriorating mortar to the correct depth and repoint with a matched mortar mix, restoring the structural integrity of the wall and dramatically improving the appearance of the brickwork at the same time.

Full Property Restoration

For properties that need more than a single trade, we bring everything together: repointing, render repair, window surrounds and a full repaint, all managed by one team under one quote. The result is a property that genuinely looks like new, without the hassle of coordinating multiple contractors.

We use quality materials throughout. No budget masonry paints or weak mortar mixes. The preparation is as important as the finish, so we never skip the steps that make the difference between a job that lasts and one that fails within a season.
