Concrete Foundations

The right base for every structure, from a garden shed to a full new build.

Solid Foundations, Done Properly

A concrete foundation is one of those things you only get one chance to do right. Get it wrong and you're looking at cracked structures, subsidence, and costly repairs down the line. At Real Developments Ltd, we bring 25 years of hands-on groundworks experience to every foundation job, no matter how large or small.

We lay concrete foundations across Sevenoaks, Tonbridge, Tunbridge Wells and the wider Kent area for a wide range of structures. Whether it's a simple slab for a garden shed, a base for a summer house or greenhouse, a garage pad, or a full strip or raft foundation for an extension or new build, we prepare, dig, form and pour to the correct specification every time.

Types of Concrete Foundations We Lay

Shed & Outbuilding Slabs

A solid, level concrete slab is the best base for any garden shed, log cabin, workshop or storage unit. We dig out to the correct depth, lay a compacted hardcore sub-base, and pour a reinforced concrete slab that won't shift, crack or flood over time.

Summer House & Garden Room Bases

Summer houses, garden offices and home studios need a properly engineered base to perform and last. We prepare the ground correctly, deal with any drainage issues, and lay a level, reinforced slab that your structure can sit on safely for decades.

Greenhouse Foundations

Greenhouses need a level, well-drained base to function properly. We lay concrete perimeter foundations or full slabs depending on the structure, ensuring the frame sits true and water drains away from the base rather than pooling around it.

Strip & Pad Foundations

Extensions, garages and new build structures require strip or pad foundations dug to engineer specification. We excavate trenches, pour concrete to the correct depth and reinforcement spec, and leave a clean, inspected foundation ready for your builder to start on.

All our concrete work is carried out using the correct mix specification for the application. We don't use watered-down mixes or cut corners on sub-base preparation. The result is a foundation that performs exactly as it should, for the full life of the structure built on top of it.
