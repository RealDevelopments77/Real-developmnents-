About Real Developments Ltd

Built on experience. Driven by pride in the work.

Who We Are

Plan Right.
Work Smart.

Real Developments Ltd has built its reputation on a simple principle: plan right, work smart. Every project we deliver is a reflection of our character, and our track record speaks for itself.

We take pride in maintaining a clean, organised site throughout, treating every property with the same respect we'd expect in our own. We only specify and supply top-tier materials, because quality is never a compromise.

Our work is carried out by a respectful, hardworking team who are committed to getting it right, and everything we do is fully guaranteed. Customer satisfaction isn't just a goal; it's the standard we hold ourselves to on every job.

Meet the Founder

Ryan Cunningham

25+ years of hands-on experience.

Ryan Cunningham founded Real Developments Ltd after more than 25 years working on the tools across all aspects of construction. His background spans large-scale foundation and excavation projects through to precision finish work, giving him a rare ability to understand a project from ground level to completion.

That breadth of experience informs every decision made on site. Ryan leads by example, attending projects personally, setting the standard for the team, and ensuring every client feels informed and confident throughout.
