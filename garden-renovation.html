Our Services

Garden Renovation

Complete garden transformations from start to finish. One team, one quote, zero hassle.

Your Garden, Completely Transformed

A full garden renovation is one of the biggest improvements you can make to a home, and one of the most complex to get right. It involves groundworks, drainage, hard landscaping, soft planting, structures and finishing details, all of which need to come together in the right order to create an outdoor space that works properly and looks outstanding.

At Real Developments Ltd, we manage the whole process. From initial clearance and levelling through to the final planting scheme, we handle every stage, which means one point of contact, one coordinated schedule, and one accountable team from start to finish. We work across Sevenoaks, Tonbridge, Tunbridge Wells and throughout Kent.

What a Full Garden Renovation Includes

Clearance & Groundworks

We start by clearing the existing garden, removing old structures, decking, overgrown planting and waste. We then level the site, address any drainage issues, and prepare a clean base for everything that follows. This stage sets up every phase that comes after, so we never rush it.

Hard Landscaping

New paving, pathways, steps, driveways and retaining walls form the bones of the garden design. We lay all surface materials on a properly prepared sub-base with correct drainage falls, whether that's sandstone, porcelain, block paving, resin, or a combination of materials that suits the property and your preferences.

Fencing & Boundary Work

New or replacement boundary fencing, garden dividers and decorative screening. We install closeboard, feather-edge, split rail, post and rail, and contemporary slatted fencing, all with proper concrete footings and a consistent, finished appearance around the entire plot.

Soft Landscaping & Planting

New turf, topsoil, planted borders, raised beds, hedging and trees. Once the hard landscaping is complete, we bring the garden to life with the planting scheme, working to create the right balance of structure, colour and seasonal interest for the garden's size and aspect.

Every renovation project starts with a proper site visit. We'll walk the garden with you, understand what you want to achieve, and put together a realistic, itemised quote. No vague estimates, no unexpected extras. Just a clear price for a clear scope of work.
